Campus Ambassador Programme


Quark, the annual technical festival of BITS Pilani, K K Birla Goa Campus, is one of the largest festivals of its kind in India. Quark’19, to be conducted in the early February, will cater a number of events and workshops to the students of engineering, pure science and management disciplines. Past Quark has attracted participation from all the leading colleges in India with a footfall of 30,000. Campus Ambassador Program offers you to be the face of Quark’19 in your college. As an integral part of the organizing team, it’s the opportunity to showcase and enhance your management and communication skills.

Responsibilities

Team Leader

CA will be solely responsible for getting participants for events and workshops for Quark from your college. CA will be the point of contact to us from the college.


Publicity Manager

CA has to publicize all events and workshops in their college as well as in social media platforms.


Event & Workshop Coordinator

CA will be the coordinator for the various regional events and Quark National Workshop conducted in their college and region.
